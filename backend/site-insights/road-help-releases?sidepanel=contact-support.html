New Diesel Price view

18.03.2026

Where in Europe are diesel prices changing the most? Our new Diesel Price view in Cost Insights answers this question by showing the latest developments in the price of diesel per litre (in Euro, including VAT) for all available European countries. Prices are shown on a weekly basis for the last 4 completed weeks, along with week-over-week and month-over-month changes. You can toggle between absolute (€) and percentage (%) display and export the data as XLS for further analysis.

Each country name links directly to Lane Overview for a deeper look into the price developments for a specific country.

This view is available to all customers of Cost Insights.

New cost updates with the new toll systems in Romania and the Netherlands in Q3 2026

07.10.2025

Our latest Cost Insights update includes the first forecast for upcoming toll systems in the Netherlands and Romania, both scheduled to come into force on July 1, 2026. Both nations are transitioning from vignette-based systems to kilometer-based tolls.

Romania's new system is expected to have a relatively low starting fee of approximately €0.03/km for a standard EURO6 truck, while the Netherlands' toll rate will be more substantial at ca. €0.201/km. For more in-depth information on these changes, please refer to our Freight Perspectives articles on the Netherlands and Romania.

The impact of these new systems is reflected in Cost Insights' newly added forecast for the third quarter of 2026, covering all lanes crossing the Netherlands and Romania. As part of this update, we have refined the historical data for some corridors to make it fully compatible with the new forecasts.

Paris as new region in France added

05.05.2025

We move the metropol area of Paris into a separate region. This should better reflect the behaviour of the market in France North which sees a lot of carriers coming back from trips to Belgium looking for loads to cover their costs.

The new region of Paris covers the following postal codes: 75, 77, 78, 91 to 95. France North will now only cover these postal code areas: 02, 08, 10, 51, 59, 60, 62, 76, 80

The main changes in Market Insights are the following:

  • We recalculated all rates and metrics for lanes going in and out of France North in Core Metrics since January 2019
  • We recalculated all rates in Rate Benchmark and Contract Rate on Demand for lanes that contain Paris or France North since January 2022

Contract rate recalculation on 10 lanes

15.02.2024

We recalculated the contract rates on 10 country-country lanes from November 2022 until February 2024. We discovered discrepancies in the data sent to us from shippers operating on our platform where diesel costs were not adequately reported in the last quarter of 2022 and the first quarter of 2023. While the overall trend of declining rates in Q1 2023 was correct, the rates themselves showed a stronger decline compared to what actually happened.

We fixed this data issue and calculated the contract rates on these lanes for the complete time period to make sure the trend development is consistent while the main time range affected was Q4 '22 and Q1 '23.
