Editing a ruleset

Autonomous Quotation automates spot quoting for carriers and logistics providers. As your business evolves, updating your quoting strategies is essential. Learn here how to edit an existing ruleset to refine your approach and maximize freight market opportunities.

Locating your ruleset

In the Rule manager, you'll find two main sections:

  • Active rulesets: These are currently being used for quoting
  • Inactive rulesets: These are temporarily deactivated Find the ruleset you want to edit in either section.

Editing the ruleset

  1. Click on the ruleset you want to edit
  2. This will open the ruleset editor
  3. Here, you can modify existing rules or add new ones

Making changes

You can edit various aspects of your ruleset, including:

  • Customer selection
  • Business rules (e.g., working hours, lead times, equipment specifications)
  • Laneways and pricing

Reviewing and saving changes

  1. After making your changes, review the estimated impact using the report
  2. If satisfied, save your changes
  3. If the ruleset was active, decide whether to keep it active or deactivate it

Tips

  • Remember that editing a ruleset may significantly impact your quoting strategy
  • Consider creating a new ruleset instead of editing an existing one if you want to test new strategies
  • Regularly review and update your rulesets to ensure they align with your current business needs
