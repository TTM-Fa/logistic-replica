Free version

You are currently using the free version of Rate on Demand. This version provides you 10 rates every month for free so you can get contract and spot rates on postal code level for a given lane and the latest month. These contract rates represent the average rates seen in the market for FTL road transports executed with ambient standard equipment on the The platform.

I have used up all rates. How can I get more?

We give you 10 rates for free every month so you can simply wait until next month. If you need more rates right now, get in touch with our Sales team who will discuss the right options with you.

What is contained in the paid version?

You will get access to the XLS upload feature that allows you to get many rates at once by simply uploading an XLS sheet with your lanes. The paid version also provides access to historic contract rates starting from January '22. Rate on Demand has a volume-based subscription which means that you buy a certain amount of rates which you can then use up any time during your contract duration.
