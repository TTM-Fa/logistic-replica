Completing tasks

Tasks help you align laneway prices with market trends and identify new opportunities to enhance your quoting strategy. By completing tasks, you ensure that your active rulesets remains optimized and up to date with the latest market insights. Tasks are renewed every week to ensure the best performance.

Locating your tasks

In the rule manager, tasks are integrated into the active rulesets section. You'll find:

  • A completion indicator in the top-right area of the active rulesets section
  • A badge with a yellow dot labeled Tasks available when tasks are pending
  • A progress bar on each ruleset, displaying the number of completed and remaining tasks

Tasks appear only when Autonomous Quotation has gathered sufficient data on quoting performance and market trends, typically after one week. Once all tasks are completed, the progress bar turns green, and the badge updates to Tasks completed.

Accessing tasks

  1. Click on open tasks of the active ruleset with tasks available
  2. This will open the ruleset view with tasks pre-filtered
  3. Review each task and take appropriate action

Task types

Tasks are categorized into two types:

  • Price updates: Identifies significant market shifts that may require price adjustments.
  • New laneways: Detects new relevant laneways added by shippers or suggests expansions based on geographic proximity to existing lanes.

Reviewing and completing tasks

Within the laneway tab, tasks are highlighted with a notification badge indicating either a Price Update or a New lane. To streamline your workflow:

  • Use the filter panel in the right action bar to display only tasks, ensuring a focused view for faster completion.
  • Tasks can be accepted (by applying a price suggestion or adding a suggested laneway) or dismissed (which also marks the task as completed).

Best practices

  • Regularly check for new tasks to keep your quoting strategy aligned with the market
  • Use the filtering option to complete tasks efficiently
  • Consider both price updates and new laneways to optimize your coverage and competitiveness

By staying up to date with tasks, you maximize your opportunities in the freight market while maintaining a well-structured quoting approach.
