Getting started

Autonomous Quotation is an advanced tool designed to automate and optimize the spot quotation process for carriers and logistics service providers. It streamlines freight quoting by leveraging real-time market data and customizable business rules to generate accurate quotes quickly and efficiently.

Take a product tour

Key Features of Autonomous Quotation

  • Automated quote generation
  • Real-time market data integration
  • Customizable business rules
  • Performance analytics

How it works

Autonomous Quotation operates through a three-step process:

  1. Order processing: The system continuously monitors incoming spot orders from Marketplace Spot platforms (Autonomous Procurement, Best Carrier, and Marketplace).
  2. Rule application: When an order matches your predefined rulesets, the system evaluates various factors including:
    • Market conditions and pricing data
    • Your operational constraints (working hours, equipment, etc.)
    • Business rules and pricing strategies
    • Available capacity (through quota management)
  3. Quote generation: If all conditions are met, the system submits a quote on your behalf. The quotes can be defined by you or you can rely on our machine learning models.

The process occurs in real-time, enabling you to respond to market opportunities around the clock while maintaining control through your customized rulesets and quotas. When new matches occur, you'll receive notifications through the same system used for manually submitted quotes.

Navigating Autonomous Quotation

Select Autonomous Quotation from the main navigation bar.

Once in the Autonomous Quotation section, you'll find these main areas:

  1. Rule manager: Here you can create and manage your quoting rules.
  2. Performance: This section provides an overview of your quoting performance, including metrics like quote volume, win rates, and revenue.
  3. Configuration: This section contains all the various company settings for your Autonomous Quotation account.
