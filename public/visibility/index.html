Connect visibility sources

Connecting your vehicles to Visibility using GPS tracking is a straightforward process that doesn't require technical expertise.

  1. Select your GPS provider: Choose your provider from the dropdown, enter integration details, and follow the instructions to connect.
  2. New provider integration: If your provider isn't listed, submit their details for integration. We'll contact them and copy you on the email.
  3. Confirmation and Vehicle Management: After successful connection, vehicles appear in the Vehicle Management section. You'll receive an email confirmation. Your fleet is now ready for allocation, enabling real-time visibility for customers.
  4. Cost-Free service: The platform provides its integration services free of charge. Please note that charges from your GPS provider for integration assistance are not included in our coverage.

Incorporating carriers into your transport operations is made straightforward and efficient through the use of the Visibility Control Center. Here’s how to do it:

  1. Send invitations: Start by entering the email addresses of your carriers and selecting their preferred language. For multiple invitations, utilize the bulk invitation feature. Each carrier will receive an individual email containing a registration link to join your network on Visibility, with you being copied on these emails.
  2. Monitor onboarding progress: Keep track of your carriers' onboarding stages directly from the Visibility Control Center, where you can also manage and resend invitations if necessary.
  3. Maintain confidentiality: Your customers will not be notified about your use of carriers. Please note, you will only see carrier vehicles when they are assigned to your transports, unless a carrier opts to share their fleet with you permanently.

I have finalized my onboarding, what’s next?

Vehicle allocation is crucial for enabling real-time tracking of shipments by assigning specific vehicles (via license plates) to transports.

Allocating in Visibility:
  1. Navigate to the "Untracked" tab, select a transport, and click "+ Allocate".
  2. Enter the license plate number and confirm. If managing tractor switches, add a second plate number for automatic updates.
  3. You have the flexibility to change, undo, or forward allocations as necessary.
Allocating on Platform Web:
  1. Go to "Assigned transports", select the transport you wish to allocate, and click "Allocate".
  2. Choose the appropriate vehicle and confirm by selecting the correct license plate number from the generated list.

Maintain your data sovereignty: Secure Sharing and Data Protection

Control your data sharing with Visibility

Protecting your data is at the heart of everything we do. You have the autonomy to manage your data-sharing preferences through the Visibility Control Center where you can actively choose which business partners to share your real-time visibility data with during transport operations. Our consent-based data sharing ensures that vehicle locations, ETAs, and statuses are shared only with to approved partners during active transports.

Secure Data Sharing: your privacy is our priority

We prioritize data privacy and adhere to GDPR guidelines, ensuring you have full control over who accesses your vehicle data. Our encryption and GDPR-compliant practices safeguard your information, enabling secure sharing with customers. Learn about our data protection methods, what data we collect, and its use in our Privacy Notice. For driver-specific privacy concerns our Driver Privacy Policy details our commitment to driver data security.
