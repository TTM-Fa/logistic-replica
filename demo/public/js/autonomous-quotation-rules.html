Welcome to the Rule manager!

Rulesets are the building blocks that determine how you quote - on which lanes, how much, and under which conditions. You can create multiple rulesets, each responsible for a specific part of your business - either a specific customer, equipment type, geography, or whatever mix is relevant for you. Learn more

Active rulesets

These rulesets are used for live quoting and define to whom, under what conditions, and how much you quote. You can have multiple active rulesets, and you can edit them without needing to deactivate them.

Inactive rulesets

This section contains your drafts or rulesets that you've temporarily inactivated. You can safely make edits - inactive rulesets are not used for quoting. You can activate them at any time if you wish.
