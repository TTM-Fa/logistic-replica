Knowledge center

Market Insights helps your company to monitor the truckload market in Europe. Build your pricing and procurement strategy based on the leading source of market intelligence for the European FTL market. We have the richest dataset in Europe and analyse up to 120.000 transports a day and up to 30 million transports per year. Leverage it – make decisions based on data, not emotions!
